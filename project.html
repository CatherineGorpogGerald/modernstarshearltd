Women Empowerment Project
×

Women empowerment project; in this area, our collectors and processors will be trained and equipped with startup materials in key areas that will help improve their livelihoods in their communities and that of their families. Some key areas will be cosmetic and pastry making and bead designs.

Environmental Protection and Sustainabilty Advocacy Project
×

Environmental protection and sustainability advocacy project: modern star sher enterprise is paramount in environmental sustainability and this calls for our advocacy projects in sustaining the environment, some of our projects are as follows; advocacy in the sustainable collection, advocacy in tree planting, and planting exercises.

Youth in Empowerment Health Project
×

Youth in environmental health project: we will organize environmental health programs for our students and the schools around our operation zones, the aim is to equip them with the knowledge on protecting their environment, and this also includes involving them in tree planting excises.

Help a child in the reading Project

This project involves the donation of reading materials to the schools in our operation areas to help improve the reading abilities of pupils. Quizzes would also be organized for these kids on regular bases and awards given.
